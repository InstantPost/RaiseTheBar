It's important to flatten the curve!
Equally important is to Raise the Bar!

We are doing our bit by developing this free to dashboard to connect all of us together.

Why ?

  1. It's important to have publicly accessible information for people who are searching for it
  2. Lack of information means lack of competition which leads to increase in prices of essential commodities
  3. Doctors and other essential services staff like doctors have no clue about cost of production so cannot negoatiate
  4. There is a huge gap between demand and supply of essential goods for medical staff, which is putting pressure on doctors to work in unsafe conditions

How ?

  1. RaiseTheBar is a digital dashboard that works as ecommerce portal for COVID suppliers to showcase their capabilities and inventory. Please use the Manufacturers tab to showcase.
  2. RaiseTheBar is a digital dashboard that works as reverse ecommerce portal for doctors to post their COVID specific requirements for suppliers and manufactures to contact them. Please use the Doctors tab to highlight your requirements.
  3. RaiseTheBar is a digital dashboard that works as reverse ecommerce portal for everyone else to post their COVID specific requirements for suppliers and manufactures to contact them. Please use the requirements tab if you not a doctor.
  4. RaiseTheBar is an initiative to share the complete production process and the cost of production for everyone to make sure doctors are aware of it and can properly decide how much they should be paying for it
  5. RaiseTheBar is an initiative to bring the best out of the 3D printing community in India to help doctors.

What ?

  1. We have setup the minimum dashboard at InstantPost and add more volunteered support to improve the dashboard. If you want to join us please post your details on the volunteers tab and we will contact as more help is needed.
  2. We have setup a minimum team of doctors and mechanical designers who are designing the minimum possible tools and equiments that can be easily 3D printed. We are adding these designs and also the ones we find opensourced on the commodities tab. If you are a mechanical designer or doctor who want to join, please post your details on the volunteers tab and we will contact you as more help is needed.
  3. We are organised the supply chain for the minimum production environment with 3D Printers, cutting machines and punching to build face shields. If you happen to be manufacturer or supplier for medical supplies or material, please post your details on the Manufacturers tab.
  4. We have setup a minimum team of doctors and mechanical designers who are designing the minimum possible tools and equiments that can be easily 3D printed. If you are a mechanical designer or doctor who want to join, please post your details on the volunteers tab and we will contact you as more help is needed.
  5. At InstantPost, we are dedicating the complete 3D printing capacity to print 1 face shield frame every 5 minutes. All these frames as part of the RaiseTheBar initiative will be donated to doctors, hospitals, police and other relevant individuals who are right now working on the frontline fighting COVID
