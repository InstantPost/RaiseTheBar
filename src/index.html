Raise The Bar

Back The Frontliners

It's important to flatten the curve,
but it is equally important to Raise the Bar

Raise The Bar strives to ignite the sense of togetherness and a combined setup to overcome the barriers imposed by COVID-19. We feel that the largest distributed but unified manufacturing setup can be built in India, if all of us join hands against a common calamity.

Joining the initiative would invoke our network of manufacturers, suppliers and volunteers to ramp up and diversify the process because the motive here is to break the chain, and we envision to achieve that by creating India’s largest chain of manufacturers and suppliers on a common platform to Raise The Bar.

A battle of numbers vs numbers

While we do everything that we can to overcome this pandemic - the numbers are increasing. And this is where our focus is to Raise the Bar by increasing our capacity to equip our heroes at the frontline with equipment that saves them from the infection as well as made available at a reasonable price.

Why?

It's important to have publicly accessible information for people who are searching for it
Lack of information means lack of competition which leads to increase in prices of essential commodities
Doctors and other essential services staff like doctors have no clue about cost of production so cannot negoatiate
There is a huge gap between demand and supply of essential goods for medical staff, which is putting pressure on doctors to work in unsafe conditions

How?

RaiseTheBar is a digital dashboard that works as ecommerce portal for COVID suppliers to showcase their capabilities and inventory. Please use the Manufacturers tab to showcase.
RaiseTheBar is a digital dashboard that works as reverse ecommerce portal for doctors to post their COVID specific requirements for suppliers and manufactures to contact them. Please use the Doctors tab to highlight your requirements.
RaiseTheBar is a digital dashboard that works as reverse ecommerce portal for everyone else to post their COVID specific requirements for suppliers and manufactures to contact them. Please use the requirements tab if you not a doctor.
RaiseTheBar is an initiative to share the complete production process and the cost of production for everyone to make sure doctors are aware of it and can properly decide how much they should be paying for it
RaiseTheBar is an initiative to bring the best out of the 3D printing community in India to help doctors.

Get the Face Shield

At Raise the Bar, we have always stood by our values of contribution, creation and cooperation to overcome every hurdle that comes our way. However, things move differently when we have bigger challenges to address and making resources accessible to those in need irrespective of the donations and availability of funds.

Thus, after putting a lot of thought behind making shields available across boundaries - we’ve zeroed down upon a basic pricing model to cater individual and organisational requirements, alike and thus - making the initiative more universal and relevant to the common interest. So, now when you stumble upon Raise the Bar - you will be able to get the shields shipped directly to your address irrespective of our emphasis upon contribution - because we understand that the disease doesn’t understand the cause nor does it waits for the same.

So, now when you post a requirement - and make a payment, be rest assured that the shields will reach you irrespective of the barriers of boundaries and logistics.

The process is continuous, but then - worth it. Moreover, we’ll be keeping a reasonable profit on every shield sold because it is important to drive the initiative in a way that is self sustaining and not dependent upon external factors for its operations.
